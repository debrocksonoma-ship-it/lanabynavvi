LANA by NAVVI

Luxury Autonomous Navigation Assistant

LANA by NAVVI is a design-led autonomous navigation and mobility concierge system. LANA integrates navigation intelligence, experiential routing, and service-oriented decision support into a cohesive luxury mobility assistant.

The LANA platform is developed as a services and licensing business, supporting premium navigation use cases, mobility experiences, and operational frameworks for consumer, commercial, and institutional partners.

Services

  • Premium autonomous navigation intelligence and routing support
  • Mobility concierge services and experiential trip planning
  • Navigation-assisted hospitality and tourism frameworks
  • Operational playbooks and service design systems
  • Licensing of navigation, branding, and service frameworks

Business Model

Revenue is generated through a combination of licensed intellectual property, service subscriptions, consulting engagements, and white-labeled operational frameworks. LANA materials and systems may be licensed for commercial, educational, and institutional use.
